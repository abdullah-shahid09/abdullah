About Evergreen Events

Your trusted partner in creating unforgettable wedding experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2013 with a passion for creating magical moments, Evergreen Events has grown to become one of the region's most trusted wedding service providers. What started as a small family business has evolved into a comprehensive wedding planning company that has orchestrated over 500 successful weddings.

Our journey began when our founder, Maria Rodriguez, realized her calling after planning her own wedding. The joy and satisfaction of creating a perfect day led her to help other couples achieve their dream weddings. Today, our team of dedicated professionals carries forward that same passion and commitment to excellence.
